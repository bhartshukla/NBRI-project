About the Portal

What is PlantVision AI?

PlantVision AI is a citizen-facing botanical identification platform that combines Plant.id's global species database with OpenRouter AI's language generation capabilities. The portal provides accurate plant identification with confidence scoring, alongside comprehensive Hindi explanations — making botanical knowledge accessible to Indian farmers, students, researchers, and nature enthusiasts across the country.

🧬

Dual AI Engine

Plant.id identifies species with high precision while OpenRouter AI generates rich contextual explanations in Hindi for Indian users.

🌍

Botanical Database

Access to over 3,00,000 plant species spanning the globe — from rare orchids to common herbs, medicinal plants and agricultural crops.

📱

Mobile Accessible

Fully responsive and accessible on any device. Upload a field photograph and receive results instantly — no app installation required.
